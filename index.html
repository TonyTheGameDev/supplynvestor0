Since 2018

Supplynvest is a platform that offers an innovative solution for businesses and individuals looking to invest in bulk shipments of goods from China. It operates on the principle of pooling resources and investing together, allowing individuals to potentially see returns on their investments as the goods are sold in markets all over the world.

The platform was founded in 2018 with the goal of making it easier and more affordable for businesses and individuals to source goods from China. By investing in bulk shipments, individuals can potentially see higher returns on their investments compared to traditional investments, while businesses can save money on sourcing and shipping expenses.

Supplynvest only offers investments in shipments of goods that have been carefully vetted to ensure their quality and demand. This focus on high-quality goods has helped the platform establish a reputation for reliability and trust among its users.

The process of investing in Supplynvest is straightforward. When a shipment becomes available, individuals can invest in it by purchasing a portion of the shipment. The goods are then purchased and shipped from China to markets all over the world. Once the goods arrive, they are sold, and the profits are divided among the investors, providing them with a return on their investment.

Supplynvest has received positive reviews from businesses and individuals who have used the platform. Its unique investment model and focus on high-quality goods have helped it establish itself as a leading player in the world of global sourcing.
